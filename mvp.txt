Pitch: Web app to track your pets, keep their information, track activity stats

MVP:
User login in/registration
Add pet(s) to Account
Update pet information (name, breed, birthday, weight, etc)
Ability to upload GPS data to get stats from walks/runs (total distance, max speed, elevation, and see route on map)
See activity totals (daily, weekly, monthly, etc)

Future Features:
frieds list to connect with pet friends
Compare daily/weekly/monthly activity with friends
Suggestions for similarly active pets
activity competitions 
Oauth for google/apple accounts
pet birthday reminders 
set privacy options
