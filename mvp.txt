Pitch: Web app to track your pets, keep their information, track stats

MVP:
User login in/registration
Add pet(s) to Account
Update pet information (name, breed, birthday, weight)
Ability to upload GPS data to get stats from walks/runs (total distance, max speed, elevation, and see route on map)

Future Features:
Furriend list to connect with pet friends
Compare daily/weekly/monthly activity with furriends
Suggestions for similarly active pets
activity competitions 
Oauth for google/apple accounts
birthday reminders
set privacy options
